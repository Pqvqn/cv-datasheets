# *[Baoule]* &mdash; Baoule (`bci`)
This datasheet is for version 23.0 of the the Mozilla Common Voice *Scripted Speech* dataset 
for Baoule (`bci`). The dataset contains 15 hours of recorded
speech (12 hours validated) from 16 speakers.

## Language
According to [Ethnologue online](https://www.ethnologue.com/language/bci/) Baoulé is a stable indigenous language of Côte d’Ivoire. It belongs to the Niger-Congo language family.
